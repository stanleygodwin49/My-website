JOLLOF RICE

a popular west african dish it is typically made with long grain rice,tomatoes,tomato paste,onions,salts,and spices.Often served with chiken or beef

EUGUSI SOUP

a rich and savory soup made with melon seeds.Often thickend with leafy vegetables like spinach or ugu[fluted pumpkin leaves]and various meat or fish.A staple in nigeria cusine

POUNDED YAM WITH EFO RIRO

pounded yam is a traditional nigeria dish made by pounding boiled yam into a smooth,dough like consistency its commonly served with efo riro, a flavorful yoruba vegetable soup.
